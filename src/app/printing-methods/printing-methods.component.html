Silkscreen Printing

Silkscreen printing is one of the most common printing method. It is the process of transferring ink through the screen and onto the apparel.

Advantages

1) Very Durable and long lasting

2) Very cost efficient for large quantities

3) Wide variety of colours ; Able to follow Pantone colour

Disadvantages

1) Expensive for small quantities

2) Expensive for multiple coloured designs

Digital & Vinyl Heat Transfer Printing

Digital & Vinyl Heat Transfer Printing is the process of using a film and transferring it onto the apparel via intense heat.

A) For Digital Heat Transfer Printing, this printing method has the ability to do up multiple coloured designs and even gradient colours.

Advantages

1) Versatile and can be printed on many material

2) Most cost friendly for multiple-coloured designs for small quantities

3) Able to print out Gradient Effect

Disadvantages

1) Not so durable

2) Colours may fade away with time

3) Recommended to hand wash. Not suitable to be placed in dryer
